Top 5 Street Workouts

Master the Streets with These Powerful Moves

Street workouts combine bodyweight exercises with the power of the environment around you. Here are the top 5 moves to get you started:

1. Pull-Ups

Find a sturdy bar, and start doing pull-ups to build upper body strength. Work your back, shoulders, and arms with this classic move.

2. Push-Ups

A great bodyweight exercise that targets the chest, shoulders, and triceps. You can do them anywhere on the streets!

3. Dips

Find a bench or parallel bars and perform dips to work your chest and triceps. It's an excellent workout for pushing strength.

4. Squats

Perfect for building lower body strength. Squats target your glutes, quads, and hamstrings, and can be done anywhere.

5. Box Jumps

If you have access to a bench or platform, box jumps are a great way to work on explosive power and coordination.

With these five street workouts, you'll build strength, stamina, and endurance while enjoying the outdoors!
