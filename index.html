About Me

Being a child of a man who's served in the military, it was always very active whenever my father used to come home. He made us work out, instilling discipline and strength in me from a young age. Around the age of 12 to 14, I learned karate, and coming from a family that worked hard in the fields, physical activity was part of our daily life.

However, work pressure and studies pushed me away from karate and other physical activities. At 17, I was just 48kg, skinny, and underconfident. Bullying in college motivated me to change my life. I joined the gym, worked hard, and built my confidence. The discipline I developed transformed me inside and out, motivating me to help others feel the same confidence and joy.

Today, I’m an ACE-certified personal trainer here to make sure you become the best version of yourself!
